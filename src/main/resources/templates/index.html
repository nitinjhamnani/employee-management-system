Energy & Petroleum (Fuel Delivery)

Fueling India through Precision Logistics.

A. Doorstep Diesel Delivery (DDD) Solutions

  • 20L PESO-Approved Jerry Cans: High-grade, anti-static, and tamper-proof solutions for small-scale delivery (Generators, Agri-use).
  • 2000L Mini Fuel Stations: Skid-mounted, "Plug-and-Play" stations for industrial sites and private fleets.
  • 6000L PESO-Approved Fuel Bowsers: Advanced mobile tankers with digital flow meters and IoT tracking for large-scale mobile refueling.

B. OMC Partnership & Lease Guidance We offer end-to-end consultancy for IOCL, HPCL, BPCL, and NAYARA fuel stations.

  • Leasing Assistance: Navigating the acquisition and operation of fully operational OMC stations.
  • Strategic Growth: Helping "FuelEnts" (Fuel Entrepreneurs) scale through professional consultancy.
