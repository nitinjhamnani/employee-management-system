1. Introduction

Welcome to the Employee Portal

The Employee Portal is your centralized platform for managing your work, tracking sales, handling customer relationships, submitting requests, and accessing your personal information. This guide will help you understand all the features and how to use them effectively.

Key Features

  • Dashboard: Overview of your daily activities, attendance, and performance metrics
  • Sales Management: Track sales, manage customers, and monitor transactions
  • Target Management: Set and track sales targets for yourself and your team
  • Request Management: Submit claims and leave requests, approve team requests
  • Employee Management: Manage team members (for managers)
  • Personal Information: Update profile, bank details, and view salary information

2. Getting Started

Login

  1. Navigate to the employee login page
  2. Enter your Employee ID (username) - This is your unique ID starting with "PGES" followed by 6 alphanumeric characters
  3. Enter your Password - Default password is the same as your Employee ID (change it after first login)
  4. Click Login
First Time Login:
  • Your default password is your Employee ID
  • You will be prompted to change your password after first login
  • Go to Profile → Reset Password to change your password

Password Reset

If you forget your password:

  1. Contact your administrator to reset your password
  2. Or use the "Reset Password" option in your profile (requires current password)

3. Dashboard

Overview

The Dashboard is your home page and provides a quick overview of:

  • Today's attendance status
  • Recent sales and commissions
  • Pending tasks
  • Sales target progress
  • Recent claims and leave requests

Key Sections

Attendance Section

  • Check In: Click to mark your attendance for the day
  • Check Out: Click when leaving for the day
  • Status: Shows your current attendance status (PRESENT, ABSENT, etc.)

Sales Summary

  • Total Sales: Your sales for the current period
  • Pending Commissions: Commissions awaiting approval
  • Approved Commissions: Commissions that have been approved

Quick Actions

  • Access frequently used features directly from the dashboard
  • View recent activities and notifications

4. Work Management

Sales Targets

My Sales Target

  • View your assigned sales targets
  • Track progress toward achieving targets
  • See commission calculations based on target achievement
  • View target periods (start and end dates)
How to View Your Sales Target:
  1. Click "My Sales Target" from the sidebar
  2. View your current target details:
    • Product assigned
    • Target units and amount
    • Achieved amount
    • Progress percentage
    • Commission rate

Manage Team Targets (For Managers)

Managers can set and manage sales targets for their team members.

How to Set Team Targets:
  1. Click "Manage Team Targets" from the sidebar
  2. Select the employee type (Promoters, Zonal Heads, etc.)
  3. Click "Set Target" for a specific employee
  4. Fill in the form:
    • Select Product
    • Enter Target Units
    • Set Period Start and End dates
    • Set Base Salary and Commission Rate
  5. Click "Save"
Note: Only employees with hierarchy levels above AREA_SALES_MANAGER can manage team targets.

Customers

View All Customers

  • See a list of all customers assigned to you
  • Search customers by name, email, or phone
  • View customer details and status

Add New Customer

  1. Click "Customers" → "Add New Customer"
  2. Fill in the required information:
    • Contact Person Name *
    • Email *
    • Phone *
    • Address
    • Area, City, State
    • Zip Code, Country
    • Status (ACTIVE, INACTIVE, POTENTIAL)
    • Notes
  3. Click "Save"

Edit Customer

  1. Go to Customers list
  2. Click "View" on the customer you want to edit
  3. Click "Edit"
  4. Update the information
  5. Click "Save"

Sales

Create New Sale

  1. Click "Sales" → "Add New Sale"
  2. Fill in the sale details:
    • Select Customer *
    • Sale Date *
    • Select Product
    • Product Name (auto-filled if product selected)
    • Quantity *
    • Unit Price *
    • Total Amount (auto-calculated)
    • Due Date
    • Description
    • Notes
  3. Click "Save"
Note: Sale ID is automatically generated (format: PGES + 6 alphanumeric characters)

View Sales List

  • See all your sales
  • Filter by date range
  • View sale status (IN_PROGRESS, COMPLETED, CANCELLED)
  • View payment status (PENDING, PARTIAL, COMPLETED)

Add Payment to Sale

  1. Go to Sales → Select a sale → "View"
  2. Click "Add Payment"
  3. Fill in payment details:
    • Payment Amount *
    • Payment Date *
    • Transaction Mode (CASH, UPI, BANK_TRANSFER) *
    • Transaction ID
    • Notes
  4. Click "Save"
Note: Payment status updates automatically based on total payments vs. sale amount.

Approve Commissions

Managers can approve commissions for sales created by their team members.

  1. Go to Sales → Select a sale → "View"
  2. View commission details
  3. Click "Approve Commission" if available
  4. Commission status changes to "APPROVED"
Note: Only Cluster Heads, Zonal Heads, and Promoters can approve commissions. Area Sales Managers cannot approve commissions.

Transactions

View All Transactions

  • See all payment transactions
  • Filter by date range
  • View transaction details including invoice number, payment amount, transaction mode, etc.

Download Invoice

  1. Go to Transactions → Select a transaction → "View"
  2. Click "Download Invoice"
  3. PDF invoice will be downloaded

5. Employee Management

Overview

Managers can add, edit, and view team members based on their hierarchy level.

Hierarchy Levels:

  • Promoter: Can manage Promoters
  • Zonal Head: Can manage Cluster Heads and Promoters
  • Cluster Head: Can manage Area Sales Managers and Promoters
  • Area Sales Manager: Cannot manage other employees

Add Team Member

  1. Click "Employee Management" → Select employee type
  2. Click "Add New [Employee Type]"
  3. Fill in the form:
    • First Name *
    • Last Name *
    • Email * (must be unique)
    • Phone * (must be unique)
    • State, City, Area *
    • Hierarchy Level (auto-set based on type)
    • Reporting Manager (select from dropdown)
    • Hire Date *
    • Status (ACTIVE/INACTIVE) *
  4. Click "Save"
Note: Employee ID (username) is automatically generated. Default password is the same as Employee ID.

Edit Team Member

  1. Go to Employee Management → Select employee type
  2. Click "View" on the employee
  3. Click "Edit"
  4. Update information (Employee ID cannot be changed)
  5. Click "Save"

6. Requests & Approvals

Claims

Submit a Claim

  1. Click "Claims" → "Submit New Claim"
  2. Fill in the claim form:
    • Claim Type * (TRAVEL, MEALS, ACCOMMODATION, etc.)
    • Description *
    • Amount *
    • Claim Date *
    • Bill Attachment (upload if available)
  3. Click "Submit"
Note: Claims are submitted for approval to your reporting manager.

View My Claims

  • See all your submitted claims
  • View claim status (PENDING, APPROVED, REJECTED)
  • View claim details and remarks

Pending Claim Approvals (For Managers)

Managers can approve or reject claims submitted by their team members.

  1. Click "Pending Claim Approvals"
  2. View list of pending claims
  3. Click "View" to see claim details
  4. Review the claim and take action:
    • Approve: Claim status changes to APPROVED
    • Reject: Claim status changes to REJECTED (add remarks)

Leave Requests

Submit Leave Request

  1. Click "Leave Requests" → "Request Leave"
  2. Fill in the leave form:
    • Leave Type * (CASUAL, SICK, EARNED, MATERNITY, PATERNITY, UNPAID)
    • Start Date *
    • End Date *
    • Number of Days (auto-calculated)
    • Reason *
  3. Click "Submit"

Cancel Leave Request

  1. Go to Leave Requests → Select a pending request → "View"
  2. Click "Cancel" if the request is still pending
  3. Leave status changes to CANCELLED

Pending Leave Approvals (For Managers)

Managers can approve or reject leave requests from their team members.

  1. Click "Pending Leave Approvals"
  2. View list of pending leave requests
  3. Click "View" to see leave details
  4. Review and approve or reject with remarks

7. Personal Information

Profile

View Profile

  1. Click on your name in the top-right corner → "My Profile"
  2. View your personal and employment information

Update Profile

  1. Go to Profile
  2. Click "Edit" or update fields directly
  3. Update information (First Name, Last Name, Email, Phone, Department, Position, State, City, Area)
  4. Click "Save"
Note: Some fields like Employee ID, Hierarchy Level, and Hire Date cannot be changed.

Reset Password

  1. Click on your name → "Reset Password"
  2. Enter:
    • Current Password *
    • New Password * (minimum 6 characters)
    • Confirm New Password *
  3. Click "Reset Password"

Bank Details

Add Bank Account

  1. Click "Bank Details" → "Add Bank Account"
  2. Fill in bank information:
    • Account Holder Name *
    • Account Number *
    • IFSC Code *
    • Bank Name *
    • Branch Name
    • Account Type (SAVINGS/CURRENT)
    • Mark as Primary (if this is your main account)
  3. Click "Save"

Salary

View Salary History

  • Click "Salary"
  • View all your salary records with details

Download Payslip

  1. Go to Salary → Select a salary record → "View"
  2. Click "Download Payslip"
  3. PDF payslip will be downloaded

8. Common Workflows

Daily Workflow

  1. Login to the portal
  2. Check In from the dashboard
  3. Review Tasks assigned to you
  4. Check Sales Target progress
  5. Work on Sales and Customers
  6. Check Out at the end of the day

Sales Workflow

  1. Add Customer (if new customer)
  2. Create Sale for the customer
  3. Add Payments as customer pays
  4. Track payment status
  5. Commission is automatically calculated
  6. Manager approves commission (if applicable)

Claim Workflow

  1. Submit Claim with bill attachment
  2. Claim goes to PENDING status
  3. Reporting manager reviews and approves/rejects
  4. If approved, admin marks as paid
  5. View payment status in claim details

Leave Workflow

  1. Submit Leave Request with dates and reason
  2. Request goes to PENDING status
  3. Reporting manager reviews and approves/rejects
  4. View approval status and remarks
  5. Cancel request if needed (if still pending)

9. Frequently Asked Questions

Login & Access

Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?
A: Contact your administrator to reset your password, or use the "Reset Password" option in your profile if you remember your current password.

Q: Can I change my Employee ID?
A: No, Employee ID is permanent and cannot be changed once created.

Q: What is my default password?
A: Your default password is the same as your Employee ID. You should change it after first login.

Sales & Customers

Q: How do I add a payment to a sale?
A: Go to Sales → View the sale → Click "Add Payment" → Fill in payment details → Save.

Q: How is commission calculated?
A: Commission is calculated based on the product's commission settings (FIXED amount or PERCENTAGE of sale amount).

Q: Who can approve commissions?
A: Only Cluster Heads, Zonal Heads, and Promoters can approve commissions. Area Sales Managers cannot approve commissions.

Claims & Leave

Q: How long does claim approval take?
A: Approval time depends on your reporting manager. You can check the status in "My Claims".

Q: Can I cancel a leave request?
A: Yes, you can cancel a leave request if it's still in PENDING status. Go to Leave Requests → View → Cancel.

Targets & Performance

Q: How do I see my sales target progress?
A: Go to "My Sales Target" from the sidebar to view your current target and progress.

Q: Can I set my own sales target?
A: No, sales targets are set by managers. Contact your reporting manager if you need to discuss your target.

Employee Management

Q: Who can manage employees?
A: Only managers (Promoters, Zonal Heads, Cluster Heads) can manage employees. Area Sales Managers cannot manage other employees.

Salary & Bank Details

Q: When will I receive my salary?
A: Salary is released by administrators. Check your salary list to see the status (PENDING or PAID).

Q: Can I have multiple bank accounts?
A: Yes, you can add multiple bank accounts. Mark one as "Primary" for salary payments.

Q: How do I download my payslip?
A: Go to Salary → View a salary record → Click "Download Payslip".

Appendix

Employee Hierarchy Levels

  1. Promoter: Entry-level sales position
  2. Area Sales Manager: Manages promoters in a specific area
  3. Cluster Head: Manages multiple Area Sales Managers
  4. Zonal Head: Manages multiple Cluster Heads
  5. Admin: System administrators (separate portal)

Status Definitions

Employee Status:

  • ACTIVE: Employee is currently active
  • INACTIVE: Employee is inactive (cannot login)

Sale Status:

  • IN_PROGRESS: Sale is ongoing
  • COMPLETED: Sale is completed
  • CANCELLED: Sale is cancelled

Payment Status:

  • PENDING: No payments received
  • PARTIAL: Partial payment received
  • COMPLETED: Full payment received

Claim/Leave Status:

  • PENDING: Awaiting approval
  • APPROVED: Approved by manager
  • REJECTED: Rejected by manager
  • CANCELLED: Cancelled by employee

Salary Status:

  • PENDING: Salary not yet released
  • PAID: Salary has been released
